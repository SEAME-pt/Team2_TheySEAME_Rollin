## RPI5

## Motors

## Back Motors

$$
\begin{aligned}
V = 6~15V \\
I_{Running} = 120mA (12V) \\
I_{Stall} = 1.2A (12V) \\
P_{Running} = 12 * 0,12 = 1,44W \\
P_{Stall} = 12 * 1,2 = 14,4W
\end{aligned}
$$

## Servo Motor

$$
\begin{aligned}
V &= 4.8V~7.2V \\
I_{Running} &= 500mA \\
I_{Stall} &= 2.5A at (6V) \\
P_{Running} &= 5V * 0,500 = 2,5W \\
P_{Stall} &= 5V * 1,2 = 6W
\end{aligned}
$$

## TouchScreen

$$
\begin{aligned}
V = 5V \\
I = 0,6A \\
P = 5 * 0,6 = 3W \\
\end{aligned}
$$
